Hi 👋, I'm wenyang, a graduate student working on NLP, more specifically on Question Answering.

There are many things can be studied:

  1. LLM Understanding: How to leverage LLM to understand human intention of asking questions, and give accurate answers. This ongoing research shows that LLMs can obtain knowledge even with rare resources, but current evaluation methods are limited.
  2. Efficient QA Systems: Current QA systems use LLM, which is computationally intensive. The challenge is to create smaller QA systems while minimizing capability loss.
  3. Addressing Biases: Language models often exhibit biases like sexism and racism, largely due to the data they're trained on. Understanding and mitigating these biases is crucial.

The field is fast-paced, and I'm always looking for collaborators to work with.
