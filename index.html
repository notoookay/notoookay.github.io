Research Interests

  1. LLM for Question Understanding: Exploring how to leverage Large Language Models to better understand human question intentions and provide accurate answers. This ongoing research investigates LLMs' ability to obtain knowledge from limited resources and improve current evaluation methods.
  2. Efficient QA Systems: Investigating methods to create smaller, more efficient QA systems while minimizing capability loss, addressing the computational intensity of current LLM-based systems.
  3. Bias in Language Models: Examining and mitigating biases (e.g., sexism, racism) in language models, which often stem from the training data and reflect societal biases present in the text authors.

The field of NLP is rapidly evolving, and I'm always eager to collaborate with fellow researchers and enthusiasts. If you're interested in working together or discussing these topics, please don't hesitate to reach out!
